Objective of the Game

A player starts on day one of the zombie apocalypse. The goal is to last as many days as possible.

Functions

Players have the option to enter a house or move to the next. The size and state of the house will give a hint as to the number of rooms and zombies inside.

When inside the house the player has the option to search different rooms. Some may contain zombies that you'll have to fend off!

Survival

Finding valuable resources such as food, ammo and survivors will increase your chances of survival.

However, the more survivors in your party, the quicker your food supply will run out.

Badges

As you progress through the game, you can earn badges for your feats. Can you unlock the coveted platinum badge?
